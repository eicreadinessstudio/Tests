Problem

Operational knowledge breaks before organisations realise it

Critical operational knowledge often remains fragmented across documents, systems, workflows, and individuals. The issue is not that information is missing. The issue is that it never becomes one trusted, governed operational view.

01

The expert leaves

The documents remain, but the judgment behind them does not. Critical logic disappears with the person who held it.

step_4_procedure — undocumented
02

Sources conflict

Procedures, tickets, notes, and systems describe the same reality differently. Nobody has mapped the contradiction.

approval_rule — 3 versions, unresolved
03

Validation is missing

Teams know something is incomplete, but not what, where, or who should confirm it. The gap stays hidden.

review_step — no reviewer defined
04

Knowledge stays siloed

Critical know-how never becomes a shared asset. It stays locked in the system or team that originally created it.

Why this matters

Not just documented. Not just searchable. Governed.

Most operational knowledge exists somewhere. The problem is not absence — it is state. Knowledge that is fragmented, unvalidated, or expert-dependent cannot be trusted, reused, or depended on at scale. Requivo moves knowledge through these states.

Fragmented
Scattered across documents, tickets, notes, and individual memory. No shared view exists.
Weakly structured
Documented in some form, but inconsistent, out of date, or contradicted by other sources.
Expert-dependent
Understood only by the people who created it. Inaccessible to the wider team without them.
Validated
Reviewed and confirmed against source evidence. Conflicts resolved. Gaps closed.
Governed
Traceable, authoritative, and maintained. One trusted version the organisation can depend on.
Reusable
Available across workflows, teams, tools, and AI systems. Knowledge that survives and compounds.
Solution

Not a search layer. A knowledge resolution system.

Requivo connects fragmented operational evidence, structures it into draft knowledge, detects conflicts and gaps, routes unresolved points to the right expert, and publishes governed outputs teams can trust.

It does not retrieve documents. It does not generate answers from memory. It does not replace your systems.

Requivo produces governed operational knowledge — validated, traceable, and reusable across workflows.
