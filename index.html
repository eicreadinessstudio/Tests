Mission

To turn fragmented enterprise evidence into governed operational knowledge that organisations can trust, maintain, and reuse.

Vision

A world in which organisations no longer depend on scattered documents and tacit memory, but operate on validated, evolving, and reusable knowledge.

Problem
Critical operational knowledge is usually not missing. It is dispersed across systems, documents, workflows, and individual experts.
Most organisations do not struggle because information is absent. They struggle because the knowledge they rely on is fragmented, hard to validate, and often concentrated in a few individuals.
01

Expertise disappears

Critical know-how remains embedded in individuals, workarounds, and tacit context instead of becoming structured and reusable across the organisation.

02

Knowledge does not truly transfer

Teams may inherit documentation, but rarely the reasoning, hierarchy, conflict resolution, and judgment that make it operationally useful.

03

Answers stay fractured

Relevant signals are distributed across documents, systems, workflows, and expert input, making them difficult to trust, maintain, and act on together.

Solution
Requivo is designed to form a governed operational knowledge layer from fragmented enterprise evidence.

Requivo is intended for environments where critical knowledge is spread across documents, systems, workflows, and expert memory.

Its role is not merely to retrieve information, but to support clearer, more validated, and more reusable operational understanding.

It should be understood as an operational knowledge system rather than a documentation platform, search interface, or generic assistant.

Core flow

Fragmented evidence → structure → governed layer

From messy source material to reusable operational knowledge with explicit structure and traceability.

Positioning

Beyond retrieval

Designed to support maintained and governed clarity, not only faster access to scattered information.

Applications

One governed layer, multiple uses

A single knowledge layer can support onboarding, operations, validation, and cross-functional decision-making.

Why this matters
Organisations do not only need better retrieval. They need a way to turn fragmented operational evidence into governed clarity.
Fragmented
Critical knowledge often starts as disconnected evidence across documents, systems, workflows, and experts.
Weakly structured
Even when information exists, it is rarely organised in a way that reflects real operational hierarchy, currentness, and reliability.
Expert-dependent
Undocumented judgment, tacit knowledge, and edge-case reasoning stay concentrated in individuals and are difficult to preserve.
Validated
Useful operational knowledge requires explicit verification and the ability to surface weak, ambiguous, or conflicting areas.
Governed
The goal is not merely a better answer, but a maintained knowledge layer with traceable structure and reusable logic.
Reusable
Once governed, operational knowledge can support multiple workflows across the organisation without depending on a few people.
Use cases
Requivo is relevant wherever critical operational know-how is fragmented, weakly documented, difficult to validate, and at risk of being lost.

Operations & manufacturing

For environments where procedures, incidents, engineering context, and maintenance know-how need to remain usable beyond individual employees and disconnected systems.

Engineering & technical services

For settings where documentation, configuration, and practical judgment must remain aligned and reusable across teams and over time.

Regulated and process-heavy environments

For organisations that require stronger traceability, better validation, and more maintainable operational knowledge than conventional tools can provide.
