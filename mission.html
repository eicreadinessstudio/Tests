Mission & Vision

Mission

To turn fragmented enterprise evidence into governed operational knowledge that organisations can trust, maintain, and reuse.

Vision

A world in which organisations no longer rely on scattered documents and tacit memory, but operate on governed, validated, and evolving knowledge.

Requivo was founded on the observation that the most critical operational knowledge in complex organisations rarely exists in a form that teams can fully trust, validate, or reuse.

It lives in procedures that haven't been reconciled with recent incidents. In expert judgment that was never formally captured. In documents that contradict each other without anyone having mapped the contradiction. In systems that describe the same operational reality differently.

Our mission is to resolve that. Not by replacing human judgment, but by connecting the evidence, structuring it into governed knowledge, surfacing what is missing or conflicting, and routing the right question to the right expert — so that organisations can operate on knowledge they can actually depend on.
