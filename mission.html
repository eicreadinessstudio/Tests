Mission & Vision

Building the governed knowledge layer organisations can depend on.

Requivo is being developed for environments where critical operational knowledge must be validated, preserved, maintained, and reused across people, workflows, systems, and AI tools.

Mission

To turn fragmented enterprise evidence into governed operational knowledge that organisations can trust, maintain, and reuse.

Vision

A world in which organisations no longer rely on scattered documents and tacit memory, but operate on governed, validated, and evolving knowledge.

Principles
The operating assumptions behind Requivo.
01

Knowledge must be governed

Operational knowledge should not remain a loose collection of files, notes, and answers. It needs structure, provenance, verification, and maintainability.

02

Experts should verify, not rewrite

Requivo is designed to ask targeted questions only where evidence is weak, conflicting, or incomplete — preserving expert judgment without creating unnecessary documentation work.

03

Reusable knowledge compounds

Once knowledge is validated and governed, it can support many downstream workflows: writing, management, verification, onboarding, compliance, and AI grounding.
